# Timeline

Definitive chronology for the project. All dates here are canon; stories and assets must align with this file. The arc spans 2029 → mid-2080s, with story present at 2080.

## Era 1: The Ban (2029–2033)

AI capability in this era: agent-capable for knowledge work, sophisticated multimodal generation, expert-level domain reasoning. Not AGI, but transformative enough to threaten mass employment.

- **2029**: AI displacement reaches political crisis. Office, media, logistics, education, and service jobs collapse across Western economies. The displacement is faster than at any point in industrial history.
- **2030**: Mass labor unrest. Coordinated strikes across sectors. Several economies stall.
- **2031**: **AI Labor Ban** passes in most Western democracies. AI cannot replace human jobs except in narrow emergency cases. Foreign holdout states refuse to sign.
- **2032**: Auditing bureaucracy stands up. Narrow non-autonomous AI assistance permitted under heavy oversight.
- **2033**: Western AI sector contracts. Labs shutter, pivot to compliance, or migrate to non-signatory states. Underground research begins.

## Era 2: The Pandemic (2034–2037)

- **2034**: A novel neurotropic respiratory virus emerges. Clinical designation **NCV-34**. Colloquially "the 34 wave" or "the 34 pandemic."
- **2035**: Global pandemic phase. Acute mortality ~0.4%. Health systems strained but not collapsed. The 2031 Ban hasn't significantly affected pandemic response, acute-care AI was never the dominant infrastructure.
- **2036**: Acute phase recedes. The virus becomes endemic. Recovery period. Epidemiological databases established to track survivors. By the end of the acute phase, roughly 60–70% of the global population has been infected at least once.
- **2037**: First clinical reports of persistent post-viral cognitive symptoms in a small subset of survivors. Treated as outlier; expected to resolve.
- **2037 onward**: NCV-34 becomes endemic. Reinfection is common. By 2080 an estimated 85–95% of living humans have been infected at some point. The virus's long-tail neurological effects accumulate across the population at a level the medical establishment will not fully characterize until decades later, see R10a in `world-rules.md`.

## Era 3: The Mistaken Diagnosis (2038–2044)

- **2038–2040**: Cognitive impairment rises across the population. Initially attributed to lifestyle factors: social isolation, sedentary life, processed food, addictive media, microplastics, environmental toxicants, sleep disorders. **Synthetic Environment Syndrome (SES)** coined in 2040 as the catch-all term.
- **2041**: Post-viral cohort develops accelerated cognitive symptoms but the connection is lost, the symptoms blend with SES presentation. Researchers raise the post-viral hypothesis; it's a fringe view.
- **2042–2043**: Healthcare system strained. Aging demographics compound. Front-page scandals about eldercare understaffing begin. Birthrates continue falling and are treated as a future problem.
- **2044**: First strong scientific evidence linking ~5–8% of cognitive decline to NCV-34 sequelae. The "**post-viral cohort**" begins to be clinically characterized. Political establishment unprepared.

## Era 4: The Recognition (2045–2049)

- **2045**: Researchers definitively connect the cognitive crisis to the 2034 pandemic. SES was real, but the *acceleration curve* was post-viral. The two compound: aging boomers + post-viral cohort + SES-impaired younger adults = three overlapping crises.
- **2046**: Foreign unbanned states publicly demonstrate their generational lead in AI capability. Political pressure builds in the West.
- **2047**: **The Brussels Consensus Report on Cognitive Decline** published. Coalition of public health agencies. Names NCV-34 sequelae as roughly one-third of the cognitive burden. Politically explosive.
- **2048**: Workforce shortage and eldercare collapse at breaking point. Elderly dying in soiled beds; nursing homes shuttering. Media coverage relentless.
- **2049**: Healthcare AI Exception debated under emergency conditions. Labor groups split. Pro-Ban hardliners (future **Restorers**) form. Pro-medicine coalition wins after months of trench warfare.

## Era 5: The Exception and the Surge (2050–2057)

- **2050**: **Healthcare AI Exception Act** passes. Scope narrow, medical research and patient care only. Not labor replacement. Not consumer use. Not military.
- **2051**: Rapid deployment begins. Foreign AI capability imported; domestic underground research surfaces. The technical surge.
- **2052**: Full industrial deployment in eldercare facilities.
- **2053**: Exception extends to mental health, sleep regulation, behavioral disorders.
- **2054**: **"Cognitive rehabilitation"** becomes a legal category, opens deeper memory, mood, and perception research authority.
- **2055–2057**: Healthcare AI accumulates the majority of legally permitted Western compute. National-emergency-priority designation. Foreign states still ahead but the gap closes.

## Era 6: Discovery (2058–2064)

- **2058**: **First stimulus-based aid discovered**, sleep induction via paired audio-visual stimulus hijacking melatonin and circadian circuits.
- **2059**: Sleep induction adopted in eldercare, insomnia clinics, prisons, emergency shelters.
- **2060**: **Multiple stimulus aids identified**, dopamine modulation (relaxation), parasympathetic activation (bodily self-repair), immune-disorder relief, pain modulation. Some paired with low-dose **psilocybin or 5-MeO-DMT derivatives** for neuroplastic amplification. Clinical, expensive, class-stratified.
- **2061–2062**: Sensory therapy becomes a dominant economic sector. Major industrialization, certifications, credentialing.
- **2063**: **First memory-recovery breakthrough**, dementia patients temporarily recognize family.
- **2064**: Researchers privately recognize that stimuli are not merely *reminding* the brain, they are directly *triggering* hidden circuits. **The mechanism is named** in classified state research.

## Era 7: Side Effects and First Deaths (2065–2069)

- **2065**: Side effects accumulate: memory gaps, compulsive humming, repeated dreams, suggestibility, emotional flattening. Dismissed as overuse. State research is *already* tracking anomalous autonomic responses in classified files.
- **2066**: **First publicly-recorded fatal accident** during a therapeutic trial. Officially recorded as cardiac failure. Cover-up begins. *FILE 003, First Cardiac Event.*
- **2067**: Internal researchers formally classify certain patterns as **"stimulus injections."** Classified under emergency security designation rather than published. *FILE 004, The Memo.*
- **2068**: First memotoxin class identified, the **Triune Stimulus**. Foreign state actors and well-resourced terror groups have been studying the same anomalies in parallel.
- **2069**: **Black Bloom attack** at Westmarket Central transit hub. First publicly-deployed memotoxin. ~14,000 deaths over six months. The attackers deployed only the visual component, embedded in a municipal art installation on transit platforms. The scent and audio components were not deployed but *exploited*: drawn from population-wide pre-encodings (ubiquitous regional cuisine aromatics, a transit chime that had circulated for a decade). The attack's central insight, recognized only in hindsight after Latent Component Inheritance was identified in 2076, was that the population was already two-thirds Marked. *FILE 005, Black Bloom.*

## Era 8: Adaptation (2070–2078)

- **2070**: Public response to Black Bloom. **Stimulus Response** police units form. First emergency goggle deployment.
- **2071**: AR censor goggles, scent masks, audio scrubbers become widespread. **Strategic Stimulus Defense Office (SSDO)** forms under national-security exception, a second AI loophole, this time for military and intelligence use. Officially defensive; privately offensive.
- **2072**: Censor AI deployed in media pipelines. Contamination problem recognized internally.
- **2073**: **Censor's Hum incident.** *FILE 006, already drafted.*
- **2074**: Exposure Classification system (Clean / Marked / Twice-Marked / Sealed / Bound) standardized.
- **2075**: Cognitive Dilution Protocols enter clinical practice.
- **2076**: Folk dilution culture takes hold among the poor, stink dens, pattern bars, drinking culture resurgence as casual prophylaxis. **Latent Component Inheritance (LCI)** formally identified after researchers reanalyze the Black Bloom victim pool.
- **2077**: Two-component memotoxins confirmed in classified state research. None deployed.
- **2078**: "Memotoxin" becomes the public term. **Cognitive Exposure Passports** deployed in major jurisdictions. **Sensory Air-Gap Architecture** introduced for high-security venues.

## Era 9: The Present (2079–2080)

- **2079**: **Verrick broadcast attack**, first publicly-confirmed LCI exploit. ~14,000 deaths over six months from one new image component plus widely-encoded legacy components (a perfume marketed since 2058; the chorus of a popular 2071 song). Separately: **the Estate incident**, three deaths inside a maximum-air-gap private residence; first proof that supply-chain attacks penetrate top-tier defense.
- **2080**: Story present., *FILES 007–012.* Marina's investigation. The Bound patient. The Boy Who Hummed. The Designer's diary.
