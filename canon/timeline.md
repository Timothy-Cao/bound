# Timeline

## Phase 1 — Labor displacement & ban (2030–2036)

- **2030**: AI replaces large numbers of workers across office, media, logistics, education, and service jobs.
- **2032**: Working-age people remain the dominant voting bloc. Anti-AI labor movements gain political power.
- **2034**: Mass strikes and unrest force governments to act.
- **2035**: Full AI Labor Ban passes. AI cannot replace human jobs except in narrow emergency cases.
- **2036**: Only light, non-autonomous AI assistance remains legal. Models are heavily audited.

## Phase 2 — Frozen progress (2037–2050)

- **2037**: AI research becomes bureaucratic. Self-improving AI, general deployment, and AI-led research are banned.
- **2038**: AI companies shrink, consolidate, or move underground.
- **2040**: **Synthetic Environment Syndrome (SES)** becomes a public-health term for decline caused by isolation, sedentary life, low-nutrient food, addictive entertainment, social breakdown.
- **2042**: Birthrates continue falling. Treated as a future problem.
- **2045**: AI ban is *politically locked, not popular*. The auditing bureaucracy is itself a major employer (~8–10M jobs) and a fierce constituency. The displacement generation still votes and remembers. No party can unwind it without political suicide. Productivity and scientific progress stagnate.
- **2048**: Pension and healthcare forecasts worsen visibly.
- **2050**: Workforce shortage hits. Not enough younger workers to support retirees, hospitals, eldercare.

## Phase 3 — Healthcare exception & sensory medicine (2052–2065)

- **2052**: Healthcare in crisis. Nursing homes and dementia-care facilities cannot hire staff. Eldercare scandals dominate news cycles.
- **2053**: Government debates AI exception. Labor groups resist.
- **2054**: **Healthcare AI Exception Act** passes. AI permitted in healthcare and eldercare under strict licensing. The wedge.
- **2055**: Exception expands to dementia care, mental-health triage, sleep regulation, patient monitoring.
- **2057**: "Cognitive rehabilitation" becomes a legal category, allowing deeper AI research into memory, mood, behavior, perception.
- **2059**: AI-designed sensory therapy becomes common in hospitals and eldercare. Light, sound, smell, room layout, rhythm, touch, images used as treatment.
- **2061**: **First sensory-memory breakthrough**: a sequence helps dementia patients temporarily recover recognition of loved ones.
- **2062**: Researchers realize the stimulus didn't *remind* the brain. It directly *triggered* hidden neural pathways. The mechanism is recognized.
- **2063**: First controlled sleep audio-image induces sleep in most people under clinical conditions.
- **2064**: Hospitals, eldercare homes, prisons, and emergency shelters adopt controlled sleep and calming stimuli.
- **2065**: Side effects appear: memory gaps, emotional flattening, compulsive humming, repeated dreams, suggestibility.

## Phase 4 — Discovery of memotoxins (2066–2070)

- **2066**: First fatal accident in a therapeutic trial. Officially recorded as cardiac failure.
- **2067**: Internal researchers classify certain patterns as **stimulus injections**.
- **2068**: First memotoxin class identified — the **Triune Stimulus**: lethal binding requires image + sound + scent encoded by the same brain.
- **2069**: **First public attack.** Image component released via hijacked livestream and viral ad — millions exposed unknowingly. Days later, scent deployed at major transit hubs. Weeks later, tone embedded in an emergency broadcast. Mass casualty event; only ~2% of exposed individuals complete binding, but that is tens of thousands of deaths.
- **2070**: Follow-up triggers continue killing late-binders for months. Public understands, finally, what happened.

## Phase 5 — Social transformation (2071–2078)

- **2071**: AR censor goggles, scent masks, audio filters become common in high-risk areas.
- **2072**: Censor AI systems trained to detect hazardous stimuli. Some become **contaminated** by the patterns they are meant to block. Defense AI becomes a vector.
- **2073**: Public screens, audio systems, advertising, scent release, synthetic media become heavily regulated.
- **2074**: **Exposure Classification system** (Clean / Marked / Twice-Marked / Sealed / Bound) becomes standard public-health framework.
- **2075**: **Cognitive Dilution Protocols** enter clinical practice — deliberate impairment of memory consolidation to prevent binding in Sealed patients.
- **2076**: Folk dilution culture takes hold among the poor: stink dens, pattern bars, drinking culture resurgence as casual prophylaxis. **Latent Component Inheritance (LCI)** formally identified after researchers reanalyze the Black Bloom victim pool and confirm that some bindings exploited common pre-existing scent exposures.
- **2078**: "Memotoxin" becomes the standard public term. Cognitive Exposure Passports and Sensory Air-Gap Architecture deployed (Generations 9–10 of the defensive arms race; see [arms-race.md](arms-race.md)).
- **2079**: **Westmarket attack** — first publicly-confirmed LCI exploit. Attacker deploys one new visual component via hijacked digital advertising; the other two components are a perfume marketed since 2058 and the chorus structure of a popular 2071 song. ~14,000 deaths over six months. Cognitive Exposure Passports become politically mandatory in several jurisdictions. Anti-recall therapy enters experimental use.
- **2079**: **Verrick Estate incident** — three deaths inside a maximum-air-gap private residence. First public proof that supply-chain attacks penetrate top-tier sensory defense.

## Phase 6 — Story present (2080)

- Society depends on restricted sensory medicine but fears perception itself.
- All known deployed memotoxins are still three-component. Triune defenses still work, barely.
- Underground and classified state research is closing in on two-component (image+sound, image+scent) memotoxins. A few confirmed two-component incidents in classified files; none yet public.
- Single-component memotoxins are theoretical; no working version exists.

## Future eras (not yet story-relevant)

- **2090s–2100s**: First confirmed two-component memotoxin in the wild. Public awareness collapses. Major restructuring of media law.
- **Far future**: Single-component memotoxins emerge. Eventually, generative memotoxins — produced on demand, personalized, undetectable.
